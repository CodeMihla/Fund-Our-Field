Background

Fund our field was established in 2022 by Moddy, Motheo, Nosipho , Namhla and Senzo .
Which are code mediators that grew up in Eastern Cape studying in the rurals with no extra mural activities .
The motive behind creating a platform called fund our field was to encourage children to be familiar with sports because they saw talent in them .

Fund our field is an NGO that aims to empower South African youth
from impoverished communities, through raising funds for their
sport fields and facilities

It is a non-oganization that aims to empower empower youth from impoverished communities through raising funds for their sports field and facilities.

Fund our field has been active for quit a while and has helped a number of communities to revamp their fields . It has been productive ever since and working productively and effectively,
which has madechange in most rural and township areas.
